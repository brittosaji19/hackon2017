About Us

hackOn is a national level hackathon in which teams of students compete in a 24 hour time span to evolve with the implementation of innovative solutions to various problems.

Instructions

Rules and Regulations

  1. Students from different colleges or same college may form a team of 3 - 5 members.
  2. Teams with female participants are required to have a mentor from their college.
  3. College ID cards are compulsory.
  4. Selected teams for the finals should pay an amount of ₹300 per head upon reaching the venue.
  5. Participants are expected to bring their own laptops and required accessories for implementing their themes.
  6. The registered teams will be further informed about the shortlisted result on or after July 27.
  7. The teams may choose their theme from any of the above-listed themes.
  8. The theme related ideas are expected to be from any stream of engineering.
  9. Decisions made by the judges and committee will be final and binding.
  10. Team members must be currently pursuing any degree.
  11. Ideas that are innovative, user-friendly, lower cost, and with good implementation level are preferred.
